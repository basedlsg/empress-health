💜 Menopause Month

Honoring transformation, resilience, and the power of women everywhere.

Dear Empress User,

Menopause is more than a life stage—it’s a transformation. As women, we deserve care that listens, tools that support us, and a community that understands.

That’s why I created Empress Health.AI — the first AI-powered health and wellness platform designed exclusively for women in perimenopause and menopause. Built with heart and purpose, this is your space for holistic menopause support, expert-informed care, and daily empowerment.

💜 What Empress Health.AI Offers

  • 24/7 AI Menopause Concierge (Ask Empress Chatbot)
  • Symptom-to-Specialist Matching
  • Guidance from Experts & Clinicians
  • Daily Affirmations & Mood Support
  • Curated Marketplace of hormone-conscious products
  • Digital Journal & Insights

🌟 Why This Matters Now

The reality: Over 60 million U.S. women are navigating menopause—most without proper support. Hot flashes, brain fog, sleep issues, and anxiety disrupt daily life, yet traditional healthcare often overlooks us.

Empress Health.AI changes that. This platform puts you back at the center with personalized, holistic, and empowering solutions.

With love and strength,

Elizabeth Koshy
Founder & CEO, Empress Health.AI

“For every woman, in every stage—because you matter.”
