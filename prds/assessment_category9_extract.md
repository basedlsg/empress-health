# Category 9 Extract — Cardiovascular & Whole-Body Energy

## Category Meta
- id: 9
- slug: cardiovascular-whole-body-energy
- title: Cardiovascular & Whole-Body Energy
- description: Menopause alters vascular tone, autonomic regulation, blood pressure variability, and mitochondrial output. Energy symptoms here are physiologic data, not just “fatigue.”

## Questions

- maxLabel: Energy feels almost completely gone

100.

103.

104.

107.
